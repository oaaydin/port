Revolutionize your application's Document Automation

Windward provides seamless integration with your C# application

‍

Windward enables C# developers to easily generate high-quality documents, spreadsheets, slide shows, & reports. We provide easy solutions for enterprises and ISVs who are looking to embed document automation systems into their own applications. Moreover, Windward’s solution is so easy and intuitive that even non-technical employees find using our designer a breeze.

How simple is it to incorporate Windward into your application? It's 11 lines of code to integrate Windward into your application. That's it. And it's this same code for every template - there is no need to write additional code for each template.

  • The creative freedom to make templates with any layout since you will be designing in the Microsoft Office platform.
  • Easily apply the data you have through JSON, SQL (ADO.NET), XML, OData, and more. And you can combine different data sources in one report.
  • Embedding Windward only takes a few seconds with the insertion of just 11 lines of code into your application.

Windward’s Solution

Windward's doc or report generator understands that not everyone involved in a business organization is skilled and knowledgeable in the technical aspects of their IT environment such as programming languages like C#. This is why Windward offers a tool that will enable the non-technical staff to create templates from applications built in a language like C#.

  1. Visually Stunning Designs
  2. Smart Document Logic
  3. Massive Time Savings
  4. Incredibly Intuitive Interface
  5. Streamlined Cost Savings
  6. Simple & Easy Integration

With Windward, your application will provide advanced easy to use document automation. And get your developers out of the business of creating document templates - let the business users create their own templates.
